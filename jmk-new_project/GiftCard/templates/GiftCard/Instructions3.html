In Part 3, you have the opportunity to receive a gift card worth £25 from one of five major apparel retailers. The retailers sell both men's and women's clothes, at prices mainly under £100.

You will select the retailer you prefer in two steps. In the first step, you will not know the name of the retailers, but you will receive information on how the retailers rank on the categories of environmental responsibility, employee welfare, animal welfare, and slow fashion. The rankings for the retailers are based on evidence from industry reports, company websites, industry initiatives and scandals reported in the media.

After making your initial choice, the names of the five retailers will be revealed. You will then have the option of retaining your original choice of retailer, or switching to one of the other retailers.

After the experiment is completed, you will be entered into a drawing with all the other participants in this experiment. We will draw one out of every ten participants at random to receive a gift card to the retailer chosen in the second step (the one in which the retailer names are known). We will send an email to all participants after the completion of the experiment listing the winning participant numbers. You are Participant {{ participant.id_in_session }} in the experiment.
