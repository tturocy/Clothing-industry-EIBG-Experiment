Ethical clothing

Some questions will ask for your view on ethical clothing. Ethical clothing is defined as clothing which is produced in a way that seeks to minimise negative impact on the environment, employees and animals. One such process if slow fashion, which is an approach to making clothes to ensure they are worn as many times as possible to reduce waste. This is achieved through more durable fabrics and designs that last more than one fashion season.
